# 🏥 V-Kes

## Project Overview

V-Kes is a digital healthcare service platform providing health assessments, training/webinars, and content management for Admin and Customer roles. During my internship as a Quality Assurance Intern at PT Humma Teknologi Indonesia, I acted as the lead tester on this project, responsible for verifying that each feature functioned correctly and provided a stable, consistent experience before real users accessed the platform.

---

## My Role

**Quality Assurance Intern - Lead Tester**
**Duration: August 12, 2025 – September 20, 2025**
**Platform: Web**
**Roles Tested: Admin, Customer**

---

## Responsibilities

- Designed 412 test cases and executed 381 test cases as lead tester.
- Executed functional, UI, and end-to-end testing on website features.
- Validated website functionality across Admin and Customer roles.
- Performed UI and responsive verification.
- Conducted regression testing after fixes.
